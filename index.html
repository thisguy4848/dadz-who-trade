The Brotherhood Code

Seven rules.
Zero exceptions.

Break one and you're out. We don't bend the code for anyone — including the guides.

  1. 01

    Family First, Markets Second

    The market will be open tomorrow. Your kid's bedtime won't.

  2. 02

    Position Size Before Setup

    We risk-size every entry before we look at the chart. No exceptions.

  3. 03

    The Wife Knows

    If you can't tell your spouse what you're risking, you're risking too much.

  4. 04

    No Lambo Talk

    We don't post P&L. We don't flex. We don't romanticize the win.

  5. 05

    Show the Losses Too

    Every loss gets dissected in the room. The system survives by being honest.

  6. 06

    Boring Survives

    A boring system run for ten years beats a brilliant one run for ten weeks.

  7. 07

    Pass It On

    What you learn here, you teach to one other dad. The brotherhood compounds.

Frequently Asked

Eight questions.
Real answers.

What platform do I actually trade on?

All trading happens on the Traders Empire platform — 200+ instruments across crypto, forex, stocks, indices, and commodities, with TradingView-powered charts and native iOS + Android apps.

How do funded accounts work?

After demonstrating discipline inside the brotherhood, members can earn access to a Traders Empire funded account. Specific terms (profit split, qualification, drawdown rules) are set by Traders Empire and shared with qualified members. Subject to platform criteria. No outcome is guaranteed.

What's the cost?

Application is free. Pricing is shared after acceptance — we don't post tiers because this isn't a course, it's a small room.

Do I need experience?

You need to have traded with real money for at least six months. We're not a beginner room.

What markets do you trade?

All five — crypto, forex, stocks, indices, commodities — through one playbook.

How much time does this take?

One focused 90-minute session per day plus the morning briefing. Built for working dads.

What if I'm not in the US?

The platform is open globally. Some country restrictions apply — check the Traders Empire register page for your jurisdiction.

Why "dads" specifically?

Because the discipline of fatherhood and the discipline of trading are the same skill, sharpened in different rooms. The brotherhood compounds when the room shares that lens.
